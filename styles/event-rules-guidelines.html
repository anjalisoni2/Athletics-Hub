Event Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before participating in any event

🎯 1. General Eligibility

  • Participants must meet the minimum age requirement.
  • Government-issued ID may be required for verification.
  • Registrations are personal and non-transferable.

📝 2. Registration Policy

  • Registration is confirmed only after successful payment.
  • All entries must be completed before the deadline.
  • Duplicate or false registrations may be cancelled.

🎁 3. Participant Entitlements (What’s Included)

  • Official event T-shirt (subject to availability).
  • Snacks and drinking water.
  • Hydration stations along the route (if applicable).
  • Participant bib number or wristband.
  • Basic medical and first-aid support.
  • Access to washrooms and rest areas.

⏰ 4. Event Check-In

  • Arrive at least 60 minutes before start time.
  • Bibs or wristbands must be worn visibly.
  • Failure to comply may lead to disqualification.

❤️ 5. Health & Medical Responsibility

  • Participants must ensure they are physically fit.
  • Anyone feeling unwell should not participate.
  • Participation is at the individual’s own risk.

🛑 6. Safety & Conduct

  • Follow instructions from officials and volunteers.
  • No cheating, harassment, or misconduct.
  • Prohibited substances are strictly forbidden.

🗺️ 7. Route & Event Compliance

  • Participants must follow the official event route.
  • Leaving the route may result in disqualification.
  • Respect fellow participants and public property.

🌦️ 8. Weather & Force Majeure

  • Events run unless conditions are unsafe.
  • Organizers may modify or cancel events if required.
  • No refunds for weather-related changes.

🎒 9. Personal Belongings

  • Participants are responsible for their belongings.
  • Organizers are not liable for loss or damage.

📸 10. Photography & Media Consent

  • Photography and videography may occur.
  • Media may be used for promotional purposes.

💰 11. Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Refunds follow the event’s refund policy.
  • No refunds after the deadline.

⚠️ 12. Liability Waiver

  • Participation is at the participant’s own risk.
  • Organizers are not responsible for injuries or losses.

📌 13. Organizer Rights

  • Organizers may update rules at any time.
  • All official decisions are final.

💳 14. Fraudulent Registration & Penalties

  • Any attempt to register using false information or impersonation is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants found violating registration rules may face immediate disqualification.
  • Organizers reserve the right to charge a penalty fee or take legal action for fraudulent activity.
  • Any refunds will be voided if fraud is detected.
Acceptance: By registering for an event, participants confirm that they have read, understood, and agreed to all rules and guidelines listed above.
